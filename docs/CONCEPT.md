# Concept

A capsule is a self-verifying digital artifact.

It contains:

* content
* identity (hash)
* authorship
* signature
* lineage reference

Unlike traditional files, a capsule does not rely on:

* servers
* databases
* external verification systems

It proves itself.

---

## Why this matters

Most digital files:

* can be copied without attribution
* can be altered without detection
* lose context over time

Capsules preserve:

* integrity
* authorship
* continuity
